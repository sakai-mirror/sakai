Using Sakai 2.0, how do I preview an assessment?

To preview an assessment as it will be seen by those taking it, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant course or project site, click Tests & Quizzes. From the Assessments page, under "Core Assessments", locate the desired test and click its title.

  2. Click Preview Assessment. Then click Begin Assessment.

  3. Click done to return to the Questions page.
