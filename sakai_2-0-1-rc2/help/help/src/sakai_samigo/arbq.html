Using Sakai 2.0, can I modify an assessment once it's published?

Once you've published an assessment, you cannot add, delete, or modify its questions, but you can change the "Delivery Dates".

You cannot revoke a published assessment. You may, however, change the "Retract Date" on an assessment so students cannot access it.

You can modify the assessment from "Core Assessments", and then publish it again.
