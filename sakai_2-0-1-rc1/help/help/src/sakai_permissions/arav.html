Using Sakai 2.0, how do I add, edit, or delete participants?

Note: You must have maintain rights for your site in order to perform any of the following functions.

On this page:

  • Adding participants
  • Editing participants
  • Removing participants

Adding participants

  1. In your site, from the menubar, click Site Info.

  2. Click Add Participants.

  3. For participants with official usernames, under "Username(s)" type the participant's username. If you wish to add more than one participant, enter each username on a separate line.

    For participants without official usernames, under "Guest(s) Email Address (external participants, e.g. jdoe@yahoo.com)", enter their email addresses, one per line.

  4. Under "Participant Roles", choose whether to give all your newly added participants the same role or different roles. Click Continue.

  5. In the next window, from the drop-down menu, select the role you would like the participant to have. If you chose to give all your participants the same role, choose that role. If you chose to give each participant a different role, use the drop-down menu next to each username and choose one. Click Continue.

    Note: Depending on your's site's configuration, roles may vary.

  6. On the next page, you have the option to automatically send email to the new participants to notify them of the site's availability. Select the appropriate radio button, and then click Continue.

  7. Click Finish.

Editing participants

  1. In your site, from the menubar, click Site Info.

  2. If you wish to change a participant's role, select the new role from the drop-down menu under "Role" next to the participant's name. You may also update a participant's status (active or inactive).

  3. Click Update.

Removing participants

  1. In your site, from the menubar, click Site Info.

  2. In the Participant List, under "Remove", check the box next to each participant you would like to remove.

  3. Click Update.
