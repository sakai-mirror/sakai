Entering or editing assignment scores in the Gradebook

Enter scores

To enter scores for assignments into the Gradebook, do the following:

  1. If you are not already on the Overview page of the Gradebook tool, click Overview near the top of the window.

  2. Locate the assignment for which you want to enter scores in the assignment list and click its title.

  3. Enter each student's score for the assignment into the box following his or her name. Scores must be greater than or equal to zero, and may contain up to two decimal places (e.g., 9.25).

  4. Click Save.

Note: Scores submitted via the Tests & QuizzesTests & Surveys tool cannot be edited in the Gradebook.

Edit scores

To change previously entered scores in the Gradebook, do the following:

  1. If you are not already on the Overview page of the Gradebook tool, click Overview near the top of the window.

  2. Locate the assignment for which you want to edit scores in the assignment list and click its title.

  3. On the Assignment Summary page, current scores for individual students for the particular assignment will appear in editable fields in the "Points" column. To edit an assignment score for a student, change the points in the appropriate field.

    If a score was entered prematurely, remove the score by deleting the points and leaving the field blank.

  4. Click Save to update the scores with your changes.
