In Sakai 2.0, how can I view available Drop Boxes and their contents?

To view the Drop Boxes available for a course site or project site, in the menubar, click Drop Box. You will see a list of Drop Boxes and/or folders containing them.

To view the contents of a Drop Box, click the black triangle next to its name.

To open a Drop Box, click the folder icon or the name of the Drop Box.
