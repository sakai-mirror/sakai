Using Sakai 2.0, how do I delete an assessment?

You can only delete assessments listed under "Core Assessments" on the Assessments page. Published assessments may not be deleted, but may be retracted.

To delete an assessment, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant course or project site, click Tests & Quizzes.

  2. From the Assessments page, under "Core Assessments", find the title of the desired assessment, and click the corresponding Remove button.

  3. You'll see a prompt asking you to confirm the deletion. If you're certain you want to delete this assessment, click Remove.
