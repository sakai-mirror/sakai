To use the Chat Room feature, follow these steps:

  1. On the menubar, click Chat Room.

  2. To see who is presently online with whom you can chat, look in the "Users in Chat" box on the right side of the screen.

  3. Type your comment or response in the text box.

  4. No one will be able to see your message until you click the Send button. Once you click Send, only an instructor or a participant who has special permission can delete your message.

  5. If you would like to discard a chat message that you have not yet sent, click the Clear button.
