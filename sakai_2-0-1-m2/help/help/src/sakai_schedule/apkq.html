The following is an overview of the Schedule feature.

What it does

Schedule allows instructors or site organizers to post items in a calendar format. The calendar has day, week, month, year, and a flat list views.

Many instructors use Schedule to post readings for each class, on the day they are due to be read.

Research, group, and department projects often use Schedule to post group deadlines.

Key concepts

Activity list

To see a list of all the activities on a calendar, click View list.

Merging schedules

All Schedules on sites you have access to are merged in your My Workspace Schedule. You can also selectively merge Schedules from sites you have access to using the Merge feature from the site you want to add other Schedules to.

Things to consider

  • Any Schedule item can have multiple attachments.

  • You can print an Adobe PDF file of any view of a Schedule by clicking the Print PDF button while in the desired view.
