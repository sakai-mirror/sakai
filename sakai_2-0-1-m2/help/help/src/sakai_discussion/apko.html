To post a discussion item (topic or response) that you previously saved as a draft, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant course or project, click Discussion.

  2. You will see a list of discussion categories and topics. Using the black triangles to expand the topics as needed, navigate to your draft posting, which will be prefixed with the term "Draft" and will occupy the same place in the discussion hierarchy as the eventual posting.

  3. Click the draft title to open the message.

  4. Once you have edited the message (if you care to do so), click Post. (You may need to scroll down to see the Post button.) You will now see that the post is no longer prefixed by "Draft"; i.e., it has been posted.
