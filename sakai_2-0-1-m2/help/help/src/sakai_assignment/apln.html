You can revise a posted assignment either before or after its open date. Follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar, click the Assignments button.

  2. From the list of assignments, check the box next to the assignment you want to revise (you can only revise one assignment at a time).

  3. Click the Revise button at the top of the window.

  4. Make your revisions in the assignment.

  5. After you have made your revisions, you can post or preview your assignment or save a draft.

    Note: If the assignment is currently open to students, you will only have the options of previewing and posting the assignment.

When students view the revised assignment, they will be notified that changes have been made.
