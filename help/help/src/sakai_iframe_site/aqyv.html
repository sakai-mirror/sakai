Sakai 2.0 Home/Worksite Information: Overview

What it does

On the homepage of each course site or project site, you will see boxes that can contain general information, recent announcements, recent discussion items, and recent chat messages.

Key concepts

Content options: You can display a webpage or text in your Worksite Information box. For instructions, see Adding content to the Worksite Information box.

Things to consider

  • Whatever you display inside Worksite Information will be viewable by everyone with access to your site.
