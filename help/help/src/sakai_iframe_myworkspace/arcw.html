Using Sakai 2.0, how do I set Message of the Day options?

On this page:

  • Setting Message of the Day options in your My Workspace
  • Setting MOTD (Message of the Day) options as a site administrator

Setting Message of the Day options in your My Workspace

In the Home tool of your My Workspace, you will see announcements from all the sites to which you belong. Setting your Message of the Day options controls how many announcements appear in this space.

To set Message of the Day options for your My Workspace, follow these steps:

  1. In My Workspace, click Home.

  2. Under "Message of the Day", click Options.

  3. You can choose to show only the subject of the recent announcements from the sites to which you belong (Show announcement subject) or show the entire text of the announcements (Show announcement Body). Additional options for displaying announcements are: how many days in the past to display announcements from, the number of announcements, and how many lines to display. Enter or choose your desired options in the boxes provided, and then click Update.

Setting MOTD options as a site administrator

To set MOTD options for a site as a site administrator, follow these steps:

Note: To use this tool, you must have administrator rights.

  1. In My Workspace, click MOTD.

  2. At the top of the screen, click Options.

  3. Choose your desired options for how you want announcements to display, and then click Update.
