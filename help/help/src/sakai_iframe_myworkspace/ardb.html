In Sakai 2.0, how do I search for a user's profile?

To search for a user's profile, follow these steps:

  1. From your My Workspace menubar, click Profiles.

  2. Under "Search for Profile", enter the last name or username of the user whose profile you would like to find. Click Search.

  3. Matching profiles will display underneath the search box. Click a user's name to view his or her profile.
