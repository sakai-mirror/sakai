Using Sakai 2.x, how to view student scores and answers to individual questions?

In the menubar of the relevant course or project site, click Tests & Quizzes. On the Assessments page, you'll see a list of Published Assessments. To see how students performed on a published assessment, click the Scores link for that assessment.

You will be able to see the following information:

  • Submission Status
  • Total Scores
  • Questions
  • Statistics

Submission Status

The Submission Status page will list each student who has taken the test, and the date the test was submitted.

Total Scores

The Total Scores for the assessment shows a table with columns listing the students, and the following information:

Date - the date they submitted the assessment,

Status - the status of the assessment (Submitted, No Submission),

Total Score - their total score,

Adj - a field for you to enter an Adjustment that will be added to their score,

Final - their Final score calculated by adding Total Score + Adj.

Comment - a text box where you can type comments that will appear to the individual student when they view their submitted test.

If you make a change to the Adj or Comment field, click the Update button to save your change.

Questions

The Questions page will list the question(s) on the test, and beneath each question will be a list of each student's answer. This can allow you to quickly compare how all students answered a specific question.

Statistics

The Statistics page shows information about the total number of submissions, the total score possible, statistical information such as the mean, median, mode, and range of the scores.

Beneath the cumulative information, each question is listed with a histogram showing the number of students who selected a particular response.
