Using Sakai 2.0, how do I create/add, edit, or delete a Syllabus item?

On this page:

  • Creating/adding a syllabus item
  • Editing a syllabus item
  • Deleting a syllabus item

Note: The Syllabus tool is available from the menubar in your course or project site. If you do not see the Syllabus tool in your site's menubar, you must add it to your site.

Creating/adding a syllabus item

To add a syllabus item, follow these steps:

  1. Click Syllabus in the menubar.

  2. Click Create/Edit. Then click New.

  3. Enter a title for your syllabus item, and then enter your content. You can copy and paste text from another file (e.g., an HTML file or a Word document).

  4. Choose whether you would like to have members of the site notified automatically via email when the item is posted.

  5. When you have finished adding your syllabus item, click Post to post it to your site, Preview to view it, Save Draft to save it as a draft, or Cancel to cancel.

  6. Click Update.

Editing a syllabus item

To edit a syllabus item, click the name of the item you wish to edit, and then follow steps 3-5 above.

Deleting a syllabus item

To delete a syllabus item, to the right of the item you wish to remove, check the box under "Remove". Click Update.
