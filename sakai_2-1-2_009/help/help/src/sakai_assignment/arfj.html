Using Sakai 2.0, how do I add attachments to an assignment I created?

To add an attachment to a new, saved, or posted assignment that you created, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar, click Assignments.

  2. To create a new assignment, click New Assignment. To work on a saved or posted assignment, click Revise.

  3. Fill in or verify any other necessary parameters (e.g., the title and due date), and then click the Add attachments button. (If you are working with an assignment that already has attachments, this will be the Add/drop attachments button.)

  4. Choose the type of attachment to add:

    • To attach a local file from your computer, click Local File... , then browse for the file, select it, and click Attach. In the next window, if you have finished attaching everything you want, click Save.
    • To attach a URL, click Website URL... . Type the URL in the box provided and click Attach. (You do not need to include the http:// prefix.) In the next window, click Save.
    • To attach an item from Resources, click From Resources... . Check the box beside the resource you would like to add and click Continue. In the next window, click Save.

  5. After you have added your attachment(s), click Post to post your assignment or Save Draft to save the changes. (If the assignment is currently open to participants, you will have only the option of posting the assignment.)
