To view the details of an activity on your schedule, follow these steps:

  1. If you aren't already viewing your schedule, from the menubar, click Schedule .
  2. To see an activity's details, click its title.
  3. To return to the calendar, click the Back button.

Note: If your schedule has more than one activity scheduled at the same time, it may be difficult to view the activities' titles. To see more details for a particular day, change your schedule's view from the View drop-down list menu to Calendar by day.
