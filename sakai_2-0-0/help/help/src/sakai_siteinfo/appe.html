Follow the steps below to edit information about a site using the Site Info tool:

  1. Log in and click the tab for the site you want to edit.

  2. In the menubar, click Site Info.

  3. Click the Edit Site Info... button.

  4. Enter your changes in the text boxes next to each field, or select the appropriate choices from the drop-down menus.

  5. To save your changes, click Continue and then at the confirmation screen, click Finish. To cancel your changes, click Cancel.
