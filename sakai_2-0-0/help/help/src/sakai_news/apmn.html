If you have the necessary permissions, you can set up a worksite to display news. You must enter a URL to an RSS news feed. An RSS feed is a specially formatted list of news articles. Note that when you set up the site, or at a later time, you can add more than one News tool.

To set up News, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar, click News.

    1. If you do not see News in your menubar, you will need to add the news tool to your site. To do this, click Site Info, then Edit Tools... . Check News, and then click Continue.

    2. In the text box, enter a title for the news feed that will appear in the left-hand menu bar.

    3. Enter the URL for the news feed.

    4. Click Continue. You will see a summary of the tools you have removed and added to the site.

    5. Click Finish.


  2. You should now see a link to the news feed with the title you provided in the menubar, along with links to the other tools on your site. To give the news feed screen itself a title, follow these steps:

  3. Click the link to the news feed in the left-hand menu bar.

  4. Click Options...  in the upper left-hand corner. This will take you to a frame called "Customizing news...".

  5. In the text box under "Frame Name", enter a name for the top of the window that will display the news feed.

  6. In the text box under "Internet Address", enter the complete URL for the RSS news feed you have chosen.

  7. Click Save.
