To duplicate or delete an assignment, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar, click the Assignments button. You will see a list of assignments.

  2. Check the box next to the assignment you want to delete or duplicate.

  3. To duplicate the assignment, click the Duplicate button at the top of the window. You will see the duplicate assignment in the "Assignment list" with the word "Copy" added to the title.

    Note: The duplicate assignment is a draft. If you want to post it, click its title and then click the Post button.

    To delete the assignment, click the Delete button at the top of the window. You will see a confirmation message prompting you either to confirm or cancel the deletion.
