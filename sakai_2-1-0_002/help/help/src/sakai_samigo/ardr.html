Using Sakai 2.0, how do I add/create, edit, or delete a template?

Note: A template consists only of assessment settings; it does not contain questions.

Creating a template

To create a template, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar, click Tests & Quizzes.

  2. On the Assessments page, click Templates.

  3. In the text box next to "Create a new template", type a name for the template, and then click Create.

  4. The fields on the next screen are the same as for any assessment.
  5. Once you've completed the fields, click Save. You will see your template listed under "Saved Templates".

Editing a template

To edit a template, from the Templates screen, under "Saved Templates", click the name of the template. Click Save to save your edits.

Removing a template

To remove a template, from the Templates screen, under "Saved Templates", click the Remove link beneath the template to be removed. Click Remove again to confirm.
