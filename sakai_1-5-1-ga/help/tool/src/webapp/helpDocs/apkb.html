To revise an announcement, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant course or project site, click Announcements.

  2. Check the box next to the announcement you want to revise, then click the Revise button.

    Note: You can only revise one announcement at a time.

  3. Make the desired changes to your announcement.

  4. After editing, you can click either Post or Preview your revised announcement, or Save a Draft. If you save your announcement as a draft, follow the above instructions to return to it and post it when you are ready. If you wish to cancel your changes, click Cancel.
