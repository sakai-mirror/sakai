To change the size of your Worksite Information box, follow these steps:

  1. Open a course or project site.

  2. If your site's homepage is not already open click the Home button in the menubar to open it.

  3. In the Worksite Information box, click the Options... button.

  4. To change the height of the box, enter a pixel value. For example, if you'd like your box to be 150 pixels in length, enter 150px .

  5. To save your changes, click Save.
