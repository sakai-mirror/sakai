Using Sakai 2.0, how do I move questions from one part to another in my assessment?

In the menubar of the relevant course or project site, click Tests & Quizzes.

To move questions from one part to another in an assessment, on the Assessments page, click the title of the assessment you wish to work on. You'll see a list of questions for that assessment. For each question you'd like to move to a different part, click Modify. Then, from the "Assign to Part" drop-down list, select the part in which you'd like that question to appear. For example, if you'd like a question to be in the first part, select 1. Your questions will be automatically sorted into the new part.
