To add or remove participants, follow these steps:

Adding participants

  1. Log into your course site or project site.

  2. In the menubar, click Site Info.

  3. Click Edit Access... near the top of the main window.

  4. Click Add Participant... .

  5. Type the participant's username. You can type more than one username.

  6. Choose whether to give all your newly added participants the same role or different roles. For more information about roles, see the Knowledge Base document Participant roles.

  7. Click Continue.

  8. In the next window, if you chose to give all your participants the same role, choose access or maintain for that role. If you chose to give each participant a different role, use the drop-down menu by each username and choose either access or maintain.

  9. Click Continue.

  10. On the next page, you have the option to automatically send email to the new participants to notify them of the site's availability. Select the appropriate radio button.

  11. Click Continue.

  12. Click Finish.

Removing participants

  1. While viewing Site Info, check the box or boxes next to the participant(s) you would like to remove.

  2. Click Remove Participant(s)...  from the menu at the top..

  3. Click Finish to remove the participant(s).
