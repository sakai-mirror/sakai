Using Sakai 2.0, how do I retract a published assessment?

To retract a published assessment, follow these steps:

  1. In the menubar of the relevant course or project site, click Tests & Quizzes.

  2. From the Assessments page, under "Published Assessments", click the Settings button that corresponds to the assessment you wish to retract.

  3. On the Settings page, click Delivery Dates to reveal this setting's options.

  4. Check the box beside Retract Date, then click the calendar icon to the right of the text box. Choose a date in the past to immediately retract the assessement, or choose a date and time at which you want the assessment to be retracted.

  5. At the bottom of the screen, click Save Settings.

The retracted assessment will no longer appear under "Published Assessment" on the My Assessments page. However, it will still be present under "Core Assessments". From there, you can click its title to modify the content of the assessment, or Settings to modify its settings. If you choose to modify the retracted assessment and republish it, remember that the newly published assessment will have a URL different from the previously published assessment. You'll need to distribute this URL to the people who will be taking the revised assessment.
